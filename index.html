Privacy Policy — The Full Count

Effective date: July 11, 2026

The Full Count (“the app,” “we,” “us”) is a pregnancy-tracking companion that reframes the journey as a nine-inning ballgame. We built it to respect one of life’s most sensitive moments, and that includes your privacy.

The short version

The app does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data. Everything you enter stays on your device.

What information the app stores

When you use the app you may enter things like your estimated due date, daily check-ins and habits, your team roster (partner, fans, bench coach), forum posts, and birth details for the Rookie Card. All of this is stored locally on your device only. It is not uploaded to any server, because the app has no server and no account system.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not create accounts or ask you to log in.
  • We do not send your data off your device.
  • We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or trackers.
  • We do not sell or share your information with anyone.

The “Dugout Deals” and “Season Tickets” features shown in this build are demonstration content. No real purchases are processed and no payment information is collected.

Test-build usage counters (“Scout’s Log”)

During beta testing, the app keeps a simple tally on your device of which features get used (for example, how many times a screen was opened). These are counts only — no names, no dates you enter, no health details, and nothing you type. This tally never leaves your device unless you choose to share it through the share sheet, and you can read exactly what it contains before sending. Deleting the app deletes the tally.

Sharing you initiate

If you choose to share a Rookie Card, a Season Ticket, or an invite, the app uses your device’s standard share sheet. Whatever you send goes to the app or person you choose (Messages, Mail, etc.). We are not involved in and do not see that content.

Deleting your data

Your data lives only on your device. You can remove it at any time by deleting the app. Reinstalling starts you fresh.

Children’s privacy

The app is intended for expecting adults and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Not medical advice

The Full Count is an educational and motivational companion, not a medical device or a source of medical advice. Always follow the guidance of your healthcare provider. In an emergency, contact your provider or local emergency services.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date above and post the revised version at the same location.
