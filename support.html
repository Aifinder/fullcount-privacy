Common questions

Where is my data stored?
Entirely on your device. The app has no account system and no server — see the privacy policy. Deleting the app deletes your data.

I got a new phone — can I transfer my season?
Data lives only on the device in this version, so it doesn't transfer automatically. If your device backup (iCloud/iTunes) included the app, restoring the backup restores your season.

My due date changed — how do I update it?
Delete and reinstall the app to restart onboarding with the new date. (An in-app way to edit the due date is on our roadmap.)

Is this medical advice?
No. The Full Count is an educational and entertainment companion. Always follow your healthcare provider's guidance, and in an emergency call your local emergency number.
