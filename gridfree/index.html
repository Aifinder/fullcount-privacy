Automatic backup power in three steps

A GridFree home standby system watches the grid 24/7 so you never have to think about it.

The grid fails

Your GridFree system senses the outage instantly — day or night, whether you're home or away on vacation.

GridFree takes over

Within seconds, the automatic transfer switch safely disconnects from the utility and your generator or Vault battery starts delivering power.

Life goes on

Lights, HVAC, refrigerators, medical devices, and Wi-Fi keep running until utility power returns — then the system hands back over and resets itself.

Frequently asked questions

Everything homeowners ask before going GridFree.

How much does a home standby generator cost?
Most whole-home installations range from $9,000 to $18,000 including the generator, transfer switch, and professional installation. Your local GridFree dealer provides an exact quote after a free in-home assessment, and financing options are available.
Generator or battery — which is right for me?
Standby generators run indefinitely on natural gas or propane and are ideal for long, multi-day outages. The GridFree Vault™ battery is silent and emission-free, perfect for shorter outages, solar pairing, and daily energy-bill savings. Many homes combine both for layered protection.
How long does installation take?
A typical home standby installation is completed in one day once permits are in place. Your certified dealer handles permitting, gas and electrical connections, inspection, and startup — and walks you through the GridFree app before they leave.
What maintenance is required?
GridFree standby generators run a brief self-test each week and report status to your phone. An annual service visit — oil, filter, and inspection — keeps your 10-year warranty in force. Vault battery systems are maintenance-free.
Can I monitor my system remotely?
Yes. The free GridFree app shows live status, outage history, fuel or charge levels, and maintenance reminders, and sends instant alerts when your system takes over during an outage.
